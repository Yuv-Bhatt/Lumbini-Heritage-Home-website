Welcome

LHH is a B&B in the Patan area of Kathmandu - founded by the young entrepreneurs Aman and Esther. This guesthouse offers wonderfully restored and cozy rooms for sleeping and relaxation, but its first and foremost intention has ever since the beginning to be a place for cross-cultural human exchanges - a place for inspiration, kindness, and friendship beyond borders.
